About the Astronomical Age Calculator

This tool allows you to calculate your age on different planets in the Solar System. Each planet has a different orbital period around the Sun, meaning your age would be different on each planet compared to your age on Earth.

How it works

Simply enter your age in Earth years and select a planet from the dropdown menu. The calculator will then convert your Earth age into the corresponding age on the selected planet using the planet's orbital period.

Orbital Periods

  • Mercury: 88 days
  • Venus: 225 days
  • Earth: 365 days
  • Mars: 687 days
  • Jupiter: 4,333 days
  • Saturn: 10,759 days
  • Uranus: 30,687 days
  • Neptune: 60,190 days
