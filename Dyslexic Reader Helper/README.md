**Empowering Readers of All Abilities**
<br>

WordWise is a Chrome extension designed to enhance the reading experience for users with dyslexia and other reading difficulties to navigate the web more comfortably. By offering customizable fonts and color options, WordWise aims to make reading online content easier and more accessible.

## Features You'll Love:

- **Customizable Fonts**: Choose from a variety of dyslexia-friendly fonts for improved readability.
- **Background Color Options**: Adjust background colors to create high-contrast themes that reduce visual strain.
